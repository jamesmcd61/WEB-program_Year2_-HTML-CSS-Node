What we do

Our work is to provide care and love to neglected and abuse animals. This can be by finding them a new home or just washing and giveing them medical help.This is with all the help of volenteers. We rehabilitate these abuse and neglected animals so that they are suitable to be replaced in a home that can provide love for them. We as a community here also go around and offer our help and resources that we can provide to others around the community. This can be by giveing washes and give vacseens to those more unfortuned and not as privledged. This is not all, we all so do events so we can raise funds to help get money for when we see an animal that despritly needs a surgery so we can get it for it. These are just some of the workings we do, please look below to get more information and see our upcoming events.
